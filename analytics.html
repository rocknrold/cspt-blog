What is the analytics platforms?

A data analytics platform is an ecosystem of services and technologies that must perform analysis on voluminous, complex, and dynamic data in order to retrieve, combine, interact with, explore, and visualize data from various sources that a company may have. A comprehensive data analysis platform includes a variety of tools with varying capabilities, such as predictive analytics and data visualization, as well as location intelligence, natural language processing, and content analytics. Its primary goal is to transform any type of data into actionable insights for real-world business outcomes.
