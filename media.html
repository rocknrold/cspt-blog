What is Media Platforms?

A media platform is specifically a platform designed and built for the purpose of delivering certain media contents to a specific audience. An effective media platform will not only provide the content for a target audience but also provide a mechanism for feedbacks and discussions.

Media platforms enables user to communicate and share content to the world such as video, pictures, and other content such as those you can see in the media platforms that is in the market right now examples of these were the tech giants like facebook, youtube( google ), quora, twitter, reddit and many more platforms.
